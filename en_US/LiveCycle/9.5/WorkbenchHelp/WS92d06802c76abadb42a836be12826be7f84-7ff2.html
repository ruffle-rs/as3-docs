This information applies to QPACs that were provided for interacting with LiveCycle Document Security 7.x.

AddSignatureField

AddSignatureField actions are replaced with invoke operations that the service for the addSignatureField_Upgrade_Subprocess process provides. The Process Upgrade Tool creates this process automatically when the upgrade is performed.

Note: The process requires the Rights Management and Encryption services to be installed and activated, in addition to the Signature service.

For information about property mappings that are common to all QPAC actions, see Common property mappings .

Encrypt For Recipients

EncryptForRecipients actions are replaced by the invoke operation on the lc8_EncRecp subprocess. This subprocess is packaged with the Process Upgrade Tool and is deployed and started automatically (if the name does not clash with an existing process) when the first upgrade is performed. If a naming conflict occurs or if the process has already been upgraded, a warning is displayed indicating that the named process is already deployed and started.
