This information applies to QPACs that were provided for interacting with LiveCycle PDF Generator 7.x.

ConvertPDF

ConvertPDF actions are replaced with invoke operations that the service for the ConvertPDF_Upgrade_Subprocess process provides. The Process Upgrade Tool creates this process automatically when the upgrade is performed.

For information about property mappings that are common to all QPAC actions, see Common property mappings .

CreatePDF

CreatePDF actions are replaced with invoke operations that the service for the CreatePDF_Upgrade_Subprocess process provides. The Process Upgrade Tool creates this process automatically when the upgrade is performed.

Note: The process requires the Distiller® service to be installed and activated, in addition to the Generate PDF service.

For information about property mappings that are common to all QPAC actions, see Common property mappings .
